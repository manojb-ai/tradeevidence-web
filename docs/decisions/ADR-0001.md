# ADR-0001: Separate Public Website and Authenticated Application

## Decision
TradeEvidence will maintain a clear separation between a public website and an authenticated application.

## Context
The product has both marketing and product-workflow needs. A split experience keeps the public-facing narrative distinct from the authenticated trading workspace.

## Alternatives
- single unified experience
- public site only
- authenticated app only

## Reason
A separate structure allows clearer product positioning, onboarding, and long-term product evolution.

## Consequences
- clearer information architecture
- easier marketing and product differentiation
- additional design and navigation coordination

## Status
Accepted
