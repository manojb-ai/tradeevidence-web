# ADR-0003: Prioritize Explainable Scoring

## Decision
TradeEvidence will prioritize explainable scoring over opaque or black-box scoring approaches.

## Context
The platform exists to support trader understanding and decision quality. Scores should be understandable and reviewable.

## Alternatives
- black-box scoring model
- AI-only scoring without explanation
- purely qualitative analysis only

## Reason
Explainable scoring aligns with the product's principles of evidence, transparency, and user-owned decisions.

## Consequences
- stronger trust and clarity
- more deliberate scoring model design
- more documentation and UI support needed

## Status
Accepted
