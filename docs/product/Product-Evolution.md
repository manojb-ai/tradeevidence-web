# Product Evolution

## Purpose

This document captures how TradeEvidence's identity has evolved as the product has been explored and designed. It is not the release roadmap, but it does explain the product's direction and the progression of its core concepts.

## Evolution

### Stock Screener

The earliest concept emphasized screening stocks and producing useful summaries for traders.

### Evidence Engine

The product evolved toward transparent evidence, explainable scores, entry zones, risks, and supporting details. This shift made analysis more understandable and less opaque.

### Decision Preparation

The focus shifted from finding stocks to preparing traders to make better decisions. This was a major conceptual change: the product became a decision-support environment rather than a simple research tool.

### Earned Confidence

The emotional outcome became clear: confidence should be earned through evidence, education, and reflection rather than blind trust or predictions. This became one of the product's core promises.

### Decision Intelligence

TradeEvidence began to organize market context, evidence, risk, user intent, and education into a cohesive decision-support process. The product became less about outputs and more about helping the user think clearly.

### Decision Workspace

The Decision Workspace emerged as the primary authenticated experience for evaluating a new opportunity or reviewing an existing position. It unified market context, evidence, counter-evidence, risk, sizing, AI support, and decision preparation into one place.

### Decision Journal

The product's long-term vision now includes a Decision Journal that preserves the decision process over time through checklists, snapshots, reviews, reflection, learning, milestones, and anniversaries.

### Community

Later phases may allow users to share Decision Stories, milestones, and lessons in privacy-conscious and educational ways. This broadens the product from a private decision tool into a learning and growth ecosystem.

## Current North Star

TradeEvidence prepares traders to make better decisions by building earned confidence through evidence, education, and reflection.
