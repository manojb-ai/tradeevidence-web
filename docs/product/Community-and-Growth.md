# Community and Growth

## Purpose

Community and growth capabilities represent a later-phase expansion of TradeEvidence. They are meant to support meaningful sharing, long-term learning, and organic product growth without undermining the platform's discipline-first philosophy.

These features are explicitly Phase 3+ ideas. They should not replace the private decision-preparation experience.

## Product Philosophy

Social sharing should emphasize:

- What the user learned
- How the decision was prepared
- How discipline contributed
- What changed over time
- What the user would repeat or improve

Profit may be celebrated, but the story should not be reduced to profit alone.

## Shareable Content

Potential future formats include:

- Decision Stories
- Learning summaries
- Milestones
- Investment anniversaries
- Market briefings
- Journal reflections
- Disciplined decision summaries
- Patient entry stories
- Thesis reviews

## Decision Stories

Decision Stories are the long-term sharing unit for community growth. They should highlight the reasoning process and lessons learned rather than simply the outcome. A strong story might include:

- Security or theme
- Holding period
- Outcome, if the user chooses to share it
- Original thesis
- Supporting evidence
- Important risks
- Biggest lesson
- What the user would repeat
- TradeEvidence attribution

## Milestones and Personal Growth

TradeEvidence may eventually support private milestones and personal growth summaries that celebrate consistency, patience, and reflection. These experiences should remain personal before they become public.

## Social Sharing and Privacy

Sharing should remain opt-in and privacy-conscious. Users should control whether amounts, percentages, securities, dates, and profile details are included. Private content remains the default, and sensitive portfolio or account data should never be exposed automatically.

## Phase 3+ Ideas

### Phase 3
- Private milestones
- Personal growth summaries
- Shareable static cards
- Investment anniversaries

### Phase 4+
- Social-media integrations
- Decision Stories
- Community learning
- Optional public journals
- Mentor mode
- Investment clubs
- Family or team workspaces

## Guardrails

The product should avoid:

- Public profit leaderboards
- “Top trader” rankings
- Encouragement of excessive risk
- Automatic publication
- Competitive mechanics that reward frequent trading

## Growth Opportunity

Well-designed Decision Stories and learning milestones can become authentic marketing. Users share something personally meaningful while introducing others to TradeEvidence's philosophy.
