# Market Summary

## Purpose
The market summary provides broader context for trading decisions.

## Users
- traders reviewing market environment
- users comparing sector and index context

## Features
- index and sector context
- market breadth indicators
- summary of broader conditions

## Future ideas
- regime summary views
- historical context snapshots
- scenario comparisons

## Dependencies
- market data
- research notes
- scoring framework

## Open Questions
- What market signals should appear by default?
- How much narrative explanation should be included?
