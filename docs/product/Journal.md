# Journal

## Purpose
The journal supports reflection, learning, and documentation of trading thinking.

## Users
- traders reviewing past decisions
- users recording assumptions and observations

## Features
- writing and organizing notes
- linking notes to scores and trades
- reflecting on outcomes

## Future ideas
- AI review of journals
- structured templates for common workflows
- search and tagging

## Dependencies
- user data model
- scoring and trade context
- AI services

## Open Questions
- Should journal entries be freeform, structured, or both?
- How should journal data be connected to reports?
