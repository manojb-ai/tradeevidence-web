# Founder Vision

## Purpose

This is the Founder's evolving notebook. It captures lessons, surprises, shifts in perspective, and deliberate exclusions that may not belong in formal specifications.

## Current Vision

TradeEvidence is a decision-intelligence platform that helps traders build earned confidence through evidence and education.

## Key Beliefs

- Market data without meaning is insufficient.
- Users interpret market information differently.
- The product should help users become informed rather than attempt to control their decisions.
- Greed and fear will remain; evidence should become an additional disciplined input.
- Strong companies can decline substantially when the broader market structure is weak.
- The quality of the decision process matters even when the eventual outcome is uncertain.
- Positive reinforcement can help users develop better habits.
- Users should be able to celebrate strong outcomes and meaningful investing milestones.

## Product We Are Building

TradeEvidence should:

- Orient the user
- Explain the market
- Highlight what deserves attention
- Challenge the thesis
- Explain risk
- Encourage position-sizing discipline
- Preserve reasoning
- Support reflection
- Celebrate growth and worthwhile outcomes

## Product We Are Not Building

TradeEvidence is not:

- A brokerage
- A signal-selling service
- A prediction oracle
- A platform that removes user ownership
- A dense enterprise system that overwhelms users on day one
- A social network optimized for showing off short-term gains

## Deliberate Approach

Build progressively:

1. Simple, inviting homepage
2. Exceptional Decision Workspace
3. Evidence and market-data ingestion
4. Decision Checklist and Journal
5. Historical validation
6. Personalization
7. Milestones
8. Community and social sharing

## Founder / Product Architect Working Model

- Founder supplies domain experience, vision, user reality, and final decisions.
- Chief Product Architect challenges assumptions, protects coherence, and converts insights into product systems.
- Copilot edits documents and implements focused, approved tasks.
