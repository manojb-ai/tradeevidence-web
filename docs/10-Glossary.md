# 10. Glossary

## Core Terms

### Evidence
Information that supports or challenges a trading idea, analysis, or view.

### Confidence
A measure of how strongly the available evidence supports a conclusion or score.

### Trend Score
A score that reflects the strength and direction of the prevailing trend.

### Setup Score
A score that reflects the quality of a trading setup based on multiple criteria.

### Momentum
The rate and persistence of recent price movement.

### Watch
An item or idea that a trader is monitoring.

### YES
A positive signal or designation indicating that a condition or criterion is present.

### NO
A negative signal or designation indicating that a condition or criterion is absent.

### Market Breadth
The health and participation of the broader market, often used to interpret the strength of individual moves.

### Relative Strength
The performance of an asset relative to its peers or a benchmark.

### Entry Zone
A price area where a trader may consider entering a position, based on analysis.

### Risk/Reward
The relationship between potential downside and potential upside in a setup.

### Squeeze
A period of low volatility that may precede a larger move.

### Cloud
A chart-based concept used in some technical frameworks to indicate a support or resistance zone.

### Journal
A structured record of observations, assumptions, reflections, and outcomes.

### Decision Support
A product capability that helps a trader evaluate evidence, research, and context without replacing their own judgment.

### Strategy Alignment
The degree to which a setup or analysis fits a trader's stated approach, assumptions, or risk considerations.

### Risk Considerations
The factors that shape how a trader evaluates potential downside, uncertainty, or exposure.

### Scanner
A tool for screening items or setups based on defined criteria.

### Portfolio
A collection of holdings or tracked positions.

### Alert
A condition-based notification or watch trigger.
