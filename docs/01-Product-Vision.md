# 01. Product Vision

## Mission
TradeEvidence exists to help self-directed traders make higher-quality decisions through transparent research, structured scoring, disciplined journaling, and clear market context.

## Vision
TradeEvidence will become a long-term operating environment for evidence-based trading analysis, where traders can organize research, understand assumptions, and review their own decision process without relying on opaque guidance.

## Problem Statement
Markets produce too much information and too little explanation. Traders need tools that help them examine evidence, understand context, and reflect on decisions without being pushed into automated conclusions.

## Target Audience
- self-directed traders
- active investors who prefer independent analysis
- traders who want structured research workflows
- users who value transparency and disciplined execution

## Core Value Proposition
TradeEvidence helps traders:
- collect and review evidence
- score ideas using explainable criteria
- document assumptions and risks
- maintain a journal of decisions and outcomes
- improve consistency over time

## Guiding Philosophy
TradeEvidence is grounded in disciplined thinking. It values evidence over noise, transparency over mystique, and user judgment over automated certainty.

## Long-term Vision
Over time, TradeEvidence aims to become a comprehensive knowledge base for trading research and decision support, helping traders build better habits, learn from outcomes, and stay aligned with their own strategy.

## Product Goals
- make research more structured and accessible
- improve clarity around why a setup is being considered
- support journal-based reflection and learning
- make scoring understandable and auditable
- strengthen trader confidence without replacing trader judgment

## Non-goals
- providing financial advice
- issuing investment recommendations
- replacing the trader's final decision
- acting as a substitute for personal research and risk management
