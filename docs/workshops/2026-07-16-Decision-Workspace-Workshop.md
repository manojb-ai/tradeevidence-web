# Decision Workspace Design Workshop

## Executive Summary
This workshop finalized Dashboard v1 as the baseline and shifted product focus to the Decision Workspace.

## Final Decisions
- Dashboard v1 approved as the homepage baseline.
- Homepage orients and educates; it does not replace research workspaces.
- Today's Briefing remains the hero section.
- 'What would you like to do today?' is the primary navigation.
- AI becomes a first-class experience.
- Decision Confidence becomes distinct from Evidence Score.
- Decision Checklist becomes the final step before acting.
- Decision Snapshot is introduced as the future mechanism for preserving reasoning.
- Decision Journal becomes the long-term vision for learning and reflection.
- Community and social sharing deferred to Phase 3+.

## Open Questions
- Decision Workspace layout
- Decision Confidence calculation
- AI interaction model inside the workspace
