# Product Philosophy

# Building Earned Confidence

TradeEvidence exists to help traders build **earned confidence through evidence and education**.

## Our Beliefs

Markets are uncertain.

No software can eliminate uncertainty.

TradeEvidence does not reduce uncertainty.

TradeEvidence helps traders **understand uncertainty**.

Confidence is earned through understanding—not prediction.

## Our Mission

We prepare traders to make better decisions.

We do not make decisions for them.

## Decision Preparation

TradeEvidence is **Decision Preparation Software**.

Every feature should prepare the trader for the next decision.

The workflow is:

Market Context

↓

Research

↓

Evidence

↓

Education

↓

Risk Assessment

↓

Capital Allocation

↓

Decision

↓

Journal

↓

Continuous Improvement

## The Decision Workspace

The application is centered around a Decision Workspace.

Its purpose is to answer:

- What is happening in the market?
- Should I be looking for trades today?
- What deserves my attention?
- Why?
- What are the risks?
- How much capital should I consider?
- Does this fit my strategy?
- What can I learn afterward?

## Product Decisions

TradeEvidence is shaped by a set of product decisions that guide both the experience and the roadmap.

- Earned Confidence: The product exists to help traders build confidence through evidence, reflection, and education rather than prediction.
- Decision Preparation Software: Every feature should prepare the trader for the next decision.
- Progressive Complexity: The experience should begin simply and become more sophisticated as users mature, moving from curated workflows to persona-based templates and then to customizable dashboards.
- Market Context is Universal: Market context is shared by all users; personas influence interpretation rather than changing the facts.
- Respectful Personalization: Personalization should guide education and workflow recommendations without overriding the trader's judgment or risk tolerance.
- Trading Profile: Users define a Trading Profile that captures experience, style, risk tolerance, horizon, and goals. This profile informs the experience while remaining flexible as the trader evolves.
- Personas drive personalization: Personas shape templates, examples, and educational guidance, helping the product feel relevant without changing market reality.
- Decision Workspace: The application centers the user experience on a Decision Workspace where evidence, risks, sizing, and learning can be organized in one place.
- Every Thesis Needs an Exit: Each opportunity should be evaluated with a clear thesis, invalidation criteria, risk plan, and position sizing logic.
- AI teaches rather than recommends: AI should reinforce understanding, explain trade-offs, and support reflection rather than make decisions on the trader's behalf.
- AI asks before answering: The system should clarify intent, context, and assumptions before responding.
- AI explains trade-offs instead of giving recommendations: When the product offers guidance, it should present multiple reasonable approaches and explain their implications.
- The trader always owns the final decision: TradeEvidence supports judgment; it does not replace it.

## Principles

- Evidence before emotion.
- Education before execution.
- Transparency before prediction.
- Explainability before automation.
- Risk awareness before opportunity.
- Capital allocation matters as much as stock selection.
- Good decisions can still produce losing trades.
- The trader always owns the final decision.

## Desired User Experience

When users close TradeEvidence, they should think:

"I understand the market."

"I know what deserves my attention."

"I understand the evidence."

"I understand the risks."

"My position size matches my risk tolerance."

"I have a plan."

"I have earned confidence."

That—not predicting winners—is the purpose of TradeEvidence.
