# 11. TradeEvidence Manifesto

Markets produce too much information and too little explanation.

TradeEvidence exists to bring structure to that noise. It is a place for traders to gather evidence, examine assumptions, evaluate context, and make decisions with greater clarity.

Evidence should be transparent. A trader should be able to see why a score exists, what factors are influencing it, and where uncertainty remains.

AI should improve understanding. It can help summarize research, explain context, and support reflection, but it should never replace the trader's judgment.

Every score should be explainable. Every insight should be reviewable. Every decision should be grounded in evidence rather than hidden logic.

Education should be built into the workflow. The product should help traders learn from their own process, strengthen their habits, and refine their thinking over time.

Transparency creates confidence. When the reasoning is visible, the trader can act with greater intention and greater discipline.
